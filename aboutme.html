Hello! I'm Zahra

zahra is passionate and deeply interested in animation, illustration, and design. She has received multiple prestigious national animation awards and contributed to a wide range of design projects. Zahra is currently completing a Master of Design and Production (Graphic Design) at the Victorian College of the Arts (VCA).

Prior to this, she graduated as an outstanding and fastest graduate from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), with a Bachelor of Design. Her passion for narrative drawing and storytelling allows her to explore various styles and techniques.

She aims to create beneficial, inspiring, and knowledgeable artworks that positively impact others.
